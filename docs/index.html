Privacy First

Your DNS. Your data. Your device.

BYODNS was built on one principle: your network activity should never leave your phone. Here's how we enforce that.

  • 📡
    Recursive on-device resolution BYODNS uses Unbound to perform full recursive DNS resolution locally — the same way enterprise resolvers work, but on your Android device.
  • 🚪
    No third-party DNS providers Your queries never pass through Google DNS, Cloudflare, or any external resolver. We don't know what you're looking up — and neither does anyone else.
  • 📵
    Zero personal data collected BYODNS collects no personal information. Optional anonymous analytics (via Firebase) only captures feature usage events — never domain names or browsing history.
  • 🔐
    Local VPN, not a proxy The VPN is a local tunnel that stays entirely on your device. It intercepts DNS packets, filters them, and responds — nothing is forwarded externally.
